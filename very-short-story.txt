The rain was falling heavily. It was like driving through a thick curtain of water. He eased off the accelerator a little. Had to be careful driving on wild nights like these. He made sure to slow down, but at the same time noticed a bright light off to the left of the road. It was so bright that he became completely blinded, and at the same he heard a faint horn in the distance. As the horn grew louder, he turned his focus back to the road, and to his surprise, woke up in his bed on a Sunday morning.
